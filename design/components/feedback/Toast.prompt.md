**Toast** — a small dark acknowledgement pill for the product's gentle confirmations. You own positioning (usually `position:fixed` top-centre) and timing.

```jsx
<Toast icon="✎">批注留下了</Toast>
<Toast loading>导入《book.epub》…</Toast>
<Toast>这章读完了 ✓，去下一章 →</Toast>
```

`loading` swaps the icon for a spinner.
