**VoiceStaff** — the 双声部 canon motif: a line of dots tracing the Pachelbel bass. Put the `first` voice (ice) at a letter's head and the `second` voice (gold, fading) at its foot; the content sits between the two voices. This fading second voice is literally *Nachklang* (the reverberation).

```jsx
<VoiceStaff voice="first" />
{/* … letter body … */}
<VoiceStaff voice="second" />
```
