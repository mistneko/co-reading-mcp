**WaxSeal** — 火漆, the signature gold wax seal (embossed with 小奏's canon note-and-echo motif). Use it to sign a letter, mark an unopened message, or anchor a page's "one ritual". `breathe` gives the unopened-letter pulse.

```jsx
<WaxSeal size="lg" breathe />          {/* unopened letter */}
<WaxSeal size="sm" broken />           {/* already read */}
<WaxSeal size="dot" />                 {/* inline author dot */}
```

Sizes: `dot` 18 · `sm` 30 · `md` 40 · `lg` 54. Never redraw the seal by hand — use this.
